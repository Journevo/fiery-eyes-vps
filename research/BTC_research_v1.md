## 2. HISTORY & ORIGIN STORY

### Creation
Bitcoin was created by the pseudonymous Satoshi Nakamoto, first described in the October 2008 whitepaper "Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System." The genesis block was mined on 3 January 2009, containing the embedded message: "The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks" — a direct commentary on the 2008 financial crisis.

### Key Milestones

| Date | Event | Impact |
|------|-------|--------|
| Jan 2009 | Genesis block mined | Birth of Bitcoin |
| May 2010 | Pizza transaction (10,000 BTC for 2 pizzas) | First real-world transaction |
| Feb 2011 | BTC reaches $1 | Parity with USD |
| Nov 2013 | First major bubble ($1,127) | Mainstream awareness begins |
| Feb 2014 | Mt. Gox collapse (850K BTC lost) | Survived first existential crisis |
| Dec 2017 | $19,783 peak (CME futures launch) | Institutional interest begins |
| Mar 2020 | COVID crash to $3,800 | Recovered in 2 months — resilience proven |
| Nov 2021 | $69,000 ATH | Institutional adoption wave (Tesla, MicroStrategy) |
| Nov 2022 | $15,479 bottom (FTX collapse) | Survived worst crisis — decentralisation validated |
| Jan 2024 | Spot BTC ETFs approved (US) | Institutional floodgate opens |
| Oct 2025 | $126,000 ATH | ETF + halving + sovereign interest peak |
| Mar 2026 | $70,800 (now) | Bear market, -44% from peak |

### Near-Death Moments
1. **Mt. Gox (2014):** Largest exchange hacked, 850K BTC stolen. Price crashed 80%. Bitcoin survived because the protocol itself was never compromised — only a centralised exchange was.
2. **China ban (2021):** Complete ban on mining and trading. 50% of hashrate went offline overnight. Network recovered within 3 months as miners relocated.
3. **FTX collapse (2022):** Second-largest exchange fraud. BTC dropped to $15,479. But Bitcoin's decentralised design meant no protocol risk — only counterparty risk for those holding on FTX.

### Team (Decentralised — no single team)
- **Satoshi Nakamoto:** Disappeared 2010. Holdings estimated 1M BTC ($70B). Never moved.
- **Bitcoin Core developers:** ~40 active contributors. No single company controls development.
- **Key maintainers:** Wladimir van der Laan (stepped down 2022), now distributed leadership.

**Score: 14/15** — The most battle-tested team in crypto is no team at all. Decentralisation IS the feature. Satoshi's disappearance is the most important thing that ever happened to Bitcoin. -1 because development pace is intentionally slow (feature, not bug, but still a factor).
