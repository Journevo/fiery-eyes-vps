## 2. HISTORY & ORIGIN STORY

### Creation
Anatoly Yakovenko published the Solana whitepaper in 2017, introducing Proof of History (PoH) — a cryptographic clock that timestamps transactions before they enter consensus. The core insight: if you solve the ordering problem first, everything else gets faster. Mainnet Beta launched March 2020.

### Key Milestones

| Date | Event | Impact |
|------|-------|--------|
| 2017 | Whitepaper published | Proof of History concept introduced |
| Apr 2018 | Seed round ($0.04/SOL) | Initial funding, $3.2M raised |
| Mar 2020 | Mainnet Beta launch | Network goes live |
| Aug 2021 | Degenerate Ape NFT launch | SOL breaks $60, NFT mania begins |

2. **FTX collapse (Nov 2022):** Alameda/FTX held hundreds of millions in SOL. Price crashed from $33 to $8 in days. The FTX estate became the largest forced seller of SOL. Most analysts wrote Solana's obituary. It rose 35x from that low.
3. **FTX estate unlocks (2023-2025):** Tens of millions of SOL unlocking periodically from bankruptcy proceedings. Each unlock created selling pressure. Solana absorbed it all.
